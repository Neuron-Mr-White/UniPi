## Design Context

### Users
Solo developer using unipi workflow tooling. Context: terminal-adjacent, checking progress on milestones and workflow documents. Quick glance, occasional deep dive. Values efficiency and clarity.

### Brand Personality
High class, elegant, technical

- **Voice**: Precise but warm, not sterile
- **Tone**: Confident, refined, intentional
- **Emotional goals**: Confidence in the process, sense of craft

### Aesthetic Direction

- **Visual tone**: Premium dark editorial — think high-end dev tool meets luxury journal
- **Theme**: Dark mode (derived from terminal context)
- **Fonts**: Source Serif 4 (display/headings), Manrope (body), JetBrains Mono (mono)
- **Color**: Warm near-black background with teal accent, amber secondary, green/yellow/red semantic
- **Anti-references**: Generic "GitHub dark" blue-on-gray, system-default sans-serifs, colored left borders, gradient text, glassmorphism

### Design Principles

1. **Typography first** — Serif headings create visual distinction; clean geometric sans body ensures readability
2. **Warmth through tint** — All neutrals carry subtle warm undertone toward the brand hue; no pure grays
3. **Restraint over decoration** — Motion is purposeful, borders are faint, cards are minimal
4. **Density without noise** — Tight internal spacing, generous external spacing; rhythm from contrast
5. **No AI slop** — No gradient text, no colored left borders, no identical card grids with icon+heading+text, no hero metric layouts
